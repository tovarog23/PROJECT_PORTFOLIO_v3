<span class="secondary-title">Course: </span> Capstone Project <br><br><br><span class="secondary-title">TYPE:</span> Office <br><br><br> <span class="secondary-title">DESCRIPTION:</span> Full design of a concrete 1 level office building taking into account both wind and seismic loading. The system is composed of ordinary reinforced moment frames as well ordinary reinforced shear walls in the weaker direction of the building to provide adequate stiffness and reduce torsional effects. All structural elements were designed applying the NTC-2017 mexican code as well as minor revisions and comparisons with ACI 318-14 code.<br><br><br><span class="secondary-title">CODES APPLIED:</span> ASCE, AISC, ACI, TMS.
