<span class="secondary-title">LOCATION:</span> Queretaro, Mexico <br><br><br><span class="secondary-title">TYPE:</span> Educational <br><br><br> <span class="secondary-title">DESCRIPTION:</span> Small educational building located inside a new german school development in the city of Queretaro. In this case, I was in charge of the design of the main structure inlcuding all details as well as designing the access building. For the main structure, a dual system of steel moment frames and braced frames was proposed, while the access was designed using confined masonry bearing/shear walls and a truss system for the roof. <br><br><br><span class="secondary-title">CODES APPLIED:</span> ASCE, AISC, AISI, ACI.
